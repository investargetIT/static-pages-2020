Company Profile

It is committed to provide full lifecycle finance advice for companies from start-ups to giants. It covers four industries: high tech, deep tech, healthcare, consumer& entertainment. Main clients include: Fosun Group, Babycare, JNBY and etc.

InvesTarget endorses innovation is the essential power for world. The mission of InvesTarget is finding ambitious entrepreneurs and empower them through fundraising and consulting.
